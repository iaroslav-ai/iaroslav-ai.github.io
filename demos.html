Heart sound classifier


Cardiovascular diseases are the cause of millions of deaths across the globe; Hence, early detection of heart disease onset is of great practical interest. In this app an AI is used which was trained to detect a presence of heart sound abnormalities in a heartbeat audio. It is assumed that the user has access to a microphone (e.g. a microphone in a smartphone) which can pick up on patient's heart sound.
